TV's 10 Best Dressed August 22-28, 1998
 
Describe your character's look:
She's not exactly concerened with fasion, but she will put together a flannel shirt with a cute, groovy skirt. This year she'll dress a little more sophisticated and sassy.
And your own look?
I'm more put together. I'm into classic Audrey Hepburn.
Are you actively involved in Claudia's look?
The costume designers are like big sisters; we work together a lot. One time I wore these knee-high boots, and they borrowed them for a couple of scenes.
My costume designer Scilla Andeen-Hernandez and supervisor Maili Lafayette are geniuses becuase…
They know what's happening.
Favorite designers?
Betsey Johnson—she's funky cool. For everday, I love Guess? and Tommy Hilfiger.
Anything you won't wear?
Bell-bottoms and polyester—yuck.
Sizing up Claudia:
"She used to always wear overalls," says costume designer Andree-Hernandez. "But she's growing up."
