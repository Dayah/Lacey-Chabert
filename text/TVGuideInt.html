TV Guide Interview April 10, 1998
 
     Family dramas are nothing new to young Lacey Chabert, who rocketed to TV stardom as Claudia Salinger on the hit series Party of Five. But the showbiz vet can still be wowed by a movie like Lost in Space: "Working with all these incredible people," she gushes, "and filming in London, the one place in the whole world I've always wanted to go—it's been like a dream! Like this big fairy tale."
Would you take a 10-year trip to outer space if you had the chance?
A cool opportunity. You know, how awesome would it be to see all these things, and not everyone gets to. But I think my reaction is a little more like Penny's. You know, everything I know to life is being taken away from me.

Audio [RealAudio]

If you did go, what would you pack?
I'd take my music, I would take my dog, I would take my clothes, 'cause the space suit thing, I've already been there, done that.
