Party of Five Cast Party November 12, 1996
 
Pof5
Folks…we now have Lacey Chabert from Party of Five. Please send your questions.
Julie
Lacey-Ok, there's this big debate? Is it you playing the violin?
Pof5
Julie - I actually play but because of sound purposes, they have to dub in the sound.
Lyn
Lacey, you do an EXCELLENT job as Claudia. Is it hard doing the really emotional scenes?
Pof5
Lyn - To do an emotional scene, I just have to be there where the character is.
Joe
Lacey: You are such a great actress. How do you like working on the show?
Pof5
Joe - I love it. Love it love it love it!
Julie
Lacey - what has been your favorite episode for your character?
Pof5
Julie - The first episode, actually. I think it shows the cahracters as individuals, but in the end it always came back to family.
Steve
Is that Jennifer I hear singing in the bkgrnd?
Pof5
Steve - Yep…that's her.
JD
lacey, i think you have a great career ahead of you, keep up the awesome work
Pof5
JD - Thank you very much. I appreciate that.
Lyn
Lacey, you and Matthew Fox have a really good rapore on the show; do you enjoy working with Matthew?
Pof5
Lyn - Matthew is like my big brother. If I'm having trouble in a scene, he's always asking if I need help. He protects me.
bourdaa
Ms. Chabert, are there any scenes that you prefer doing over others?
Pof5
Bourdaa - I love comedy but my favorite is more dramatic scenes.
Julie
Lacey, any off-season plans for movies, etc?
Pof5
Julie - I really want to get into feature films. Hopefully, this hiatus.
Lyn
Lacey, how much "off time" do you get to do the things you love to do? (besides being Claudia, of course…)
Pof5
Lyn - I have a large family and weekends are for family time.
Steve
lacey how young werre u when u started?
Pof5
Steve - The first thing I did was Les Miserables on Broadway. I was 9.
IggyIp
Lacey, how was it to be on Jay Leno?
Pof5
Iggylp - It was a lot of fun.
Lyn
Lacey, do you spend much off camera time with the cast? I heard you go to each other's houses for dinner on occasion.
Pof5
Lyn - We're all like a family. I know it sounds lame, but I have no other way to put it. We love each other.
Bill
Do you have a significant other, Lacey?
Pof5
Bill - Yes.
Pof5
Matt and Scott protect me like their little sister.
Lyn
Lacey, do people stop you all the time out in the "real world" and ask for your autograph? Do you like it, or does it become an invasion of privacy?
Pof5
Lyn - I enjoy talking to people. As the show gets more popular, they seem to know more about the characters.
Julie
Lacey - what about schooling, how do you work that in?
Pof5
Julie - I have a teacher on the set in a classroom. And I have 3 hours of schooling a day, and an hour of homework.
Bill
Do you enjoy reading fan mail?
Pof5
Bill - I do. I read all of my mail and I respond to as much of it as best as I can.
Lyn
Lacey, do you personally surf the net for your own leisure and check out what's going on in the POF web site?
Pof5
Lyn - Yes. We had chat sessions on America Online. We've all done that a couple of times.
Julie
Lacey, how do your siblings deal with your stardom?
Pof5
Julie - At home I'm just Lacey.
bourdaa
Lacey, do you think that Claud and Owen should interact more often?
Pof5
Bourdaa - Owen is getting as big as me.
Bill
How many Brothers and Sisters do you have I am one of six. Party of Six.
Pof5
Bill - My older sisters.
mocha
LACEY, what were you in before pof?
Pof5
Mocha - Les Miserables and All My Children.
Greg
Lacey, who did you play in Les Miserables?
Pof5
Young Cosette and young Eponine.
Lyn
Lacey, do you do much travelling? I live in Minnesota, but I'd love to come to L.A. and see a taping of POF!
Pof5
Lyn - Yes. I've been to 39 of the 50 states. I love to travel to see interesting places.
Pof5
Lacey says goodbye. It was fun talking to everyone. Thanks!
