VIBE 1998
 
Sinbad
Please say hello to the very talented and lovely Lacey Chabert.
Lacey Chabert
[Enters]
Sinbad
You look cute!
Lacey Chabert
Thank you.
Sinbad
You got a little haircut?
Lacey Chabert
A little haircut. Did a little movie.
Sinbad
Uh huh. What movie was this?
Lacey Chabert
Lost in Space.
Sinbad
Oh! You guys all remember Lost in Space? You guys did a remake of Lost in Space?
Lacey Chabert
Yes, we did a remake of Lost in Space. Filmed it in London for 4 months.
Sinbad
Excuse me!
Lacey Chabert
That's where I got the shoes. Kinda stole 'em. I mean, I didn't steal 'em but they just kinda happen—
Sinbad
Your character stole 'em.
Lacey Chabert
Yeah, my character stole 'em and they happened to show up in my suitcase. You know, I'll give 'em back in the sequel.
Sinbad
So was it fun?
Lacey Chabert
Yeah, it was really great.
Sinbad
Good, good, good. Now, what about Claudia, our favorite TV personality on the show? Is there any new changes for her besides the cut?
Lacey Chabert
Well, Claudia, you know, she's trying to have a new image—have a little, you know, trendiness to her. And she's going to high school, and so she's trying out for the cheerleading squad hoping that she'll be, you know, the head mas—the head cheerleader and umm, she goes with great expectations to find out that she's the mascot, a possum!
Sinbad
A possum?
Lacey Chabert
A possum. So I get to walk around in a possum costume for three episodes.
Sinbad
Is it hot?
Lacey Chabert
It's very hot! Yeah, it's not even a cute mascot.
Sinbad
Actually we have a picture of it. Could we show the picture of her in the little possum?

[Clip Plays]

Sinbad
Wow, you're always doing different things on the show. That's what we like about your character. You even kissed a guy.
Lacey Chabert
I had to have my first kiss in front of, like, a hundred people.
Sinbad
That was your first kiss?
Lacey Chabert
It was my first kiss, yeah. I didn't know what to do. So my sisters told me to, like, practice on a pillow, you know? But it didn't kiss me back so I didn't know what to expect. But it was good practice I guess.
Sinbad
Uh huh. Did you uhh—did you ever have to like—did you go to anybody else for practice?
Lacey Chabert
Well, no.
Sinbad
Like uh, you know, friends in your neighborhood?
Lacey Chabert
Yeah, I just knocked on their doors, "Hey! Wanna kiss?"
Sinbad
"Hey, come on! I gotta kiss for money! Come on! Somebody have a kiss for free!" So, umm, tell me uh—tell me uh, what is, umm, making your character drive so hard who she is? I mean, you're a 14-year-old girl but your character has so much, so many dimensions. Where does that come from?
Lacey Chabert
Well for me, to have to do stuff like that, you have to play, you know, emotional scenes. I mean the only way I can be there and really get into the character is if I'm her. You know?
Sinbad
Is she really close to who you are for real?
Lacey Chabert
Uh, I think we're pretty different. I think, umm you know, Claudia not having parents is not very grounded at sometimes and she takes on more responsibility than she should have to. So I think we're different in that way. And, you know, I do have really great family.
Sinbad
Now, you have a boyfriend?
Lacey Chabert
Ahem! Umm—
Sinbad
Oh, am I getting too personal? I'm sorry—
Lacey Chabert
No, no. I DO. Actually, when I was in [London], I had a little European romance—
Sinbad
What's his name?
Lacey Chabert
Alexander. When I was in London—
Sinbad
You had a little English boy?
Lacey Chabert
He's actually not English.
Sinbad
Oh, okay.
Lacey Chabert
He lives in New York though. Like, long distance.
Sinbad
Oh, that long distance stuff, yeah.
Lacey Chabert
But umm, and I had my first kiss and everything.
Sinbad
Is he an actor too?
Lacey Chabert
No, he's not but he's actually the SON of an actor. But I can't say who he is, you know.
Sinbad
Sure! Tell us!
Lacey Chabert
Oh! No! No! No!
Sinbad
So tell us about Lost in Space.
Lacey Chabert
Well, Lost in Space is just like, you know, the old show was except much more serious and you know, Gary Oldman and William Hurt are in it. It was amazing working with them and it was so much fun and it was so different from Part of Five, you know. We'd be staring out at green screens, watching things explode and, you know, on a spaceship. And so it was fun.
Sinbad
A lot of special effects?
Lacey Chabert
Tons of special effects! It's supposed to, like, make special effects history. I think.
Sinbad
Are you into special effects? Did you see like Terminator 2 and like, uh, what's the new movie, Spawn?
Lacey Chabert
I haven't seen that yet but I want to. Yeah, I had to deal with this little yellow lizard-monkey called Blarp.
Sinbad
What's his name?
Lacey Chabert
Blarp.
Sinbad
Blarp?
Lacey Chabert
That's his name right now. It might change.
Sinbad
Uh huh. When I was younger, we had a— it was an episode of a— it was a show called, uh, it was Lost in Space? Remember the show with Jim Neighbors? Dorse? My Favorite Martian?! He was like half dog, half horse? Y'all don't remember? Am I the—am I the oldest one here? Alright, forget y'all! So you're from where now?
Lacey Chabert
I'm from Mississippi. And my dad is French Cajun. He's from Louisiana.
Sinbad
Can you do an accent? Can you talk like a French Cajun?
Lacey Chabert
I'm not very good with the accent but I like the food. The food is good.
Sinbad
The food is jammin'. Vivica [Fox] was talkin' about that soul food stuff. She likes that food too.
Lacey Chabert
Yeah, I want to open up a Cajun food restaurant, you know, called Chabert's.
Sinbad
Chabert's Soul Food!
Lacey Chabert
Yeah, exactly. True Cajun cuisine. You're all invited!
Sinbad
You all may come down! Well, thank you for coming down to the show to be with us.
Lacey Chabert
Thank you.
Sinbad
One more time for Lacey Chabert please! Be sure to check out Lacey Chabert on Fox's Party of Five, Nickelodeon's Wild Thornberries and this fall in Lost in Space!
